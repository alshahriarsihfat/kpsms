# Khan Pharmacy Staff Management & Payroll System (KPSMS)

A modern, scalable, and enterprise-grade Pharmacy Staff Management & Payroll System built with Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and Supabase.

---

## Project Overview

KPSMS is designed to digitize and automate staff management operations of Khan Pharmacy.

The system manages:

- Employee Management
- Attendance Tracking
- Shift Management
- Counter Assignment
- Leave Management
- Task Management
- Notice Board
- Payroll Management
- Salary Payment
- Reports & Analytics

---

## Technology Stack

| Technology | Purpose |
